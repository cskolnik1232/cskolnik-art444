Hi! My name is

I am a 23 year old artist from San Diego, California. I am currently majoring in Graphic Design with a minor in Women’s and Gender Studies at San Diego State University. I love exploring all kinds of art and working with different materials. My artistic hobbies include drawing, painting, ceramics, and making jewelry.

I’m especially passionate about graphic design and visual branding. I’m skilled in Adobe applications including Photoshop and Illustrator, and enjoy digitally developing creative ideas. I’m interested in pursuing a career in brand identity, designing across multiple platforms including websites, apps, and social media.
